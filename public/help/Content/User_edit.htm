Editing your Account

This topic should open from the context-sensitive linkuser_edit.

Your page displays the following details about your account:

  • Displays your email address
  • List of current Workspaces
  • Shows recent activity

Managing your Account

Select your account from the list to perform the following tasks:

Edit Profile: This allows you to modify all the information you entered when you created the user account.

Delete User: This allows you to delete your account. You must transfer all active workspaces and instances before you can delete it.

Change password: This allows you to change your password. This link is only visible if you are exploring your own account.
